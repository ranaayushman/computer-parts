Output Devices

Output devices are hardware components that receive data from the computer and convert it into a form understandable by users. They allow the computer to communicate with us.

Common Output Devices

  • Monitor – Displays text, images, and videos from the computer.
  • Printer – Produces a physical copy of digital documents or images.
  • Speakers – Output sound such as music, alerts, or spoken words.
  • Headphones – Provide audio output directly to the user.
  • Projector – Projects computer display onto a larger screen.
